API Documentation for Frontend Developer

Two main endpoints:

  1. Get statistics of all runes: /api/runes/stats
  2. Get general statistics: /api/runes/totals

Below is detailed information about each endpoint, including example responses and field descriptions.

Field Descriptions

Main Rune Fields

  • rune_name (string): The name of the rune.
  • rune_number (number): The unique number of the rune.
  • symbol (string | null): The symbol of the rune, if available.
  • divisibility (number): The number of decimal places the rune can be divided into.
  • image_uri (string | null): URL of the rune‚Äôs image, if available.

price (object): Price Information

  • market_cap (string): Market capitalization of the rune.
  • floor_unit_price_value (string): Current floor price per unit of the rune.
  • delta_floor_1d (string): Change in floor price over the last 1 day.
  • delta_floor_7d (string): Change in floor price over the last 7 days.
  • delta_floor_30d (string): Change in floor price over the last 30 days.

volume (object): Trading Volume Information

  • volume_1h (string): Trading volume over the last hour.
  • volume_1d (string): Trading volume over the last 24 hours.
  • volume_7d (string): Trading volume over the last 7 days.
  • volume_30d (string): Trading volume over the last 30 days.
  • volume_all (string): Total trading volume since inception.

transactions (object): Transaction Information

  • total_txns (number): Total number of transactions.
  • txn_count (number): Number of transactions over the last period (implementation-dependent).
  • txn_count_1d (number): Number of transactions over the last 24 hours.
  • txn_count_7d (number): Number of transactions over the last 7 days.
  • txn_count_30d (number): Number of transactions over the last 30 days.

holder_count (object): Holder Information

  • holder_count (number): Total number of rune holders.
  • smart_holders_count (number): Number of ‚Äúsmart‚Äù holders (definition may vary).

pending_count_tx (number): Pending Transactions Count

  • Number of transactions that are pending or in processing.

Notes

  • Numeric values presented as strings (e.g., "market_cap": "114.6051452720") should be converted to numbers on the frontend for mathematical operations.
  • Fields may have null values; ensure your application handles such cases appropriately.
  • Price changes (delta_floor_1d, delta_floor_7d, delta_floor_30d) are typically represented in relative units or percentages.

2. Get General Statistics

  • URL: /api/runes/totals
  • Method: GET
  • Description: Returns general statistics across all runes.

Sample Request

GET /api/runes/totals HTTP/1.1 Host: api.mosaikbtc.com

Sample Response

{
  "total_volume_1h": "10.1234000000",
  "total_volume_24h": "100.9876000000",
  "total_market_cap": "1000000.2300000000",
  "total_wallets_holders": 150000,
  "total_active_wallets_24h": 5000,
  "created_at": "2024-10-15T13:17:38.080Z",
  "updated_at": "2024-10-15T13:17:38.080Z"
}

Field Descriptions

  • total_volume_1h (string): Total trading volume of all runes over the last hour.
  • total_volume_24h (string): Total trading volume of all runes over the last 24 hours.
  • total_market_cap (string): Total market capitalization of all runes.
  • total_wallets_holders (number): Total number of wallets holding runes.
  • total_active_wallets_24h (number): Total number of active wallets over the last 24 hours
